Literafy

Picture this: You're knee-deep in research ideas, drowning in a sea of PDFs, and your bibliography resembles a chaotic Jackson Pollock painting. Enter Literafy, the bibliographic bard. It reads your mind (basically your research title), weaves a comprehensive literature review spanning 20 research ideas, and elegantly cites every source. Also, gives a PDF that's more organized than a Swiss watchmaker's workshop.

IdeoSynth

Every researcher knows the agony of a lackluster title. IdeoSynth transforms rough ideas into golden titles. Got a half-baked hypothesis about time-traveling squirrels? IdeoSynth serves up ten alternatives, each more captivating than the last. Suddenly, your paper on "Rodent Temporal Mechanics" becomes "Chrono-Rodentology: Navigating the Continuum."
